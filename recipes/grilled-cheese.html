Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a recipe taken from allrecipes.com. It appears to be a pretty simple sandwhich. I think all three of the cheeses are acceptable without passing into "fancy grilled cheese" territory. However, the base is rye bread, which I think is just lunacy.

To be completley transparent, I originally intended to use exclusively soup recipes for this site, but as it turns out, soup has a lot of ingredients! So this recipe was chosen for its small number of ingredients. While I appreciate the practice of the unordered list, after the fourth list item, I'm like, "yeah, I get it."

Also, the original recipe titles this sandwhich, "Cheesy Grilled Cheese". I find the redundancy infuriating and, thusly, have omitted it for my purposes here.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice muenster cheese
  • 1 slice provolone cheese
  • 2 slices rye bread

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven's broiler.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread, and place butter side down on a baking sheet. Place cheese slices on top of each piece of bread.
  3. Broil until cheese is bubbly and slightly brown. Remove from the oven, and press the two pieces of bread together cheese to cheese.
